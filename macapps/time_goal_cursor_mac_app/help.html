Welcome to Cursor Tracker! 🌙

🚀 Quick Start

This app attaches a subtle time and goal tracker to your cursor that stays visible across ALL windows.

⌨️ Shortcuts (Global)

Option + T : Increment Thanks Counter

Option + S : Increment Sorry Counter

⚙️ Settings

Right-click the Moon Icon in your macOS menu bar to:

  • Change your current Focus Goal
  • Reset your daily counters
  • Enable/Disable the tracker

🔒 Permissions

If shortcuts aren't working, please ensure Accessibility is enabled for this app in System Settings > Privacy & Security.
